Getting to Lodge Ohno
Lodge Ohno is very easy to find, if you get to the Akakura Kanko Ski Area. The hotel is just below and to the south of the ski area (across the green bridge, and only about one minutes' walk from the slopes) and is right on Prefectural Road 39.

Coming by train: Exit at Myokokogen Station. Most travelers will be coming from Nagano, either transferring from the Nagano Shinkansen, or from Nagaya on the Chuo Line. It's only about 40 minutes from Nagano to Myokokogen. At the station, a taxi is your best bet. There are buses leaving for Akakura, but the walk is a bit far (fine if you don't have a lot of gear). Once here, it's easy to access the shuttle buses that travel between Akakura Onsen Ski Area, Akakura Kanko Ski Resort, Ikenotaira Ski Resort and Suginohara Ski Resort.

By car: Exit at the Myokokogen Interchange of the Joshinetsu Expressway; from there, it's just 5 to 10 minutes to Lodge Ohno (turn left at the Toyohashi signal, the first major intersection after exiting the freeway and turing left).
