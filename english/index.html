Welcome to Lodge Ohno

Lodge Ohno is a family-owned pension in Shin-Akakura, Myoko, just seconds away from the Akakura Kanko Ski Area. We pride ourselves on providing a warm, friendly, affordable style of service—Lodge Ohno is a place to relax and enjoy all the natural beauty and fun of Myoko.

Enjoy our 24-hour furo bath, in-house password-free WiFi service, and evening drinks and snacks in the fireplace area.

Take a look inside, and if you have any questions, please send us an email to info_e@lodgeohno.com. We’ll get back to you right away.
