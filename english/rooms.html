Accommodations
Lodge Ohno has 13 rooms (12 Japanese-style; one Western); enjoy the special experience of sleeping on futons in tatami-mat rooms.
One of our Japanese guest rooms

We can host a maximum of about 64 people. There's nothing fancy about our rooms; the Japanese style is more about making your own fun in a simple space! For relaxing, talking or having a drink together, come to our fireplace/lounge space attached to the dining room. We do have an evening beverage and snack menu available as well.

Relax in the ofuro, our bath
We have 13 Japanese rooms, and one Western Twin room. All rooms are equipped with TV, and heaters/air conditioners. The ofuro, or bath, can be used 24 hours a day. A ski drying room, tune-up bench and other facilities are also available for skiers and boarders.

Ski tuning and storage room
The hotel has parking for 25 cars, and can handle vehicles as large as tour buses.

For large groups, schools etc., please contact us for special rates, etc.

Check-in/out
Generally, check-in is from 3 p.m.; if rooms are vacant, early check-in is possible at no extra charge. The parking lot and furo (bath) may be used after check-out at no additional cost.
